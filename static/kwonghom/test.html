Kwong "Tommy" Hom

October 24, 1929 – October 11, 2015

Please join us in a celebration of his life on Saturday, October 24, 2015



A memorial service will be held at 10 am at Alhambra True Light Presbyterian Church, 20 W. Commonwealth Ave., Alhambra, CA. Lunch will follow at NBC Seafood Restaurant, 404 S. Atlantic Blvd., Monterey Park, CA.

Kwong “Tommy” passed away on October 11, 2015 due to complications of pneumonia. He is survived by his wife, Betty Hom; children, Nancy (Dick) Lem, Robert Hom, Natalie (Danny) Hum, Noreen (Brian) Soria; grandchildren, Mark and Scott Lem, Melissa Charpied and Alex Hom, Noelle, Nadine and Nicholas Hum, Nathan and Jonathan Soria; and great granddaughter, Cadence Charpied.

(Note: Limited parking for elderly/handicapped only at Alhambra True Light Presbyterian Church site. Additional parking is available across the street at the Alhambra Superior Court building on 1st Street.)
